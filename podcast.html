Podcast:Huh?

By: Sean Acceus, Kaliyah Lendore, Camryn Nelson, Manal Shihadeh

This podcast explores and discusses university-related stories sourced from Reddit. With a focus on experiences and challenges, this podcast is a platform for insightful conversations. Hosts, Kaliyah Lendore, Sean Acceus, Camryn Nelson, and Manal Shihadeh share their opinions and perspectives on the scenarios discussed. Their goal is to inform, entertain, and engage the audience with relatable and thought-provoking content.
